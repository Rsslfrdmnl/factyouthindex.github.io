BEA ALONZO, DOMINIC ROQUE HIWALAY NA AYON KAY BOY ABUNDA

"Pagtatapos ng Isang Kabanata: Bea Alonzo at Dominic Roque, Opisyal na Naghiwalay – Ano ang hinaharap?"

KINUMPIRMA mismo ng talent manager at tinaguriang King of Talk na si Boy Abunda na hiwalay na sina Bea Alonzo at Dominic Roque.

Sa kanyang talk show na “Fast Talk with Boy Abunda” ay diretsahang kinumpirma ng talent manager ang kumakalat na balita ukol sa celebrity couple. Aniya, matagal na niyang kakilala at kaibigan ang dalawa kaya siya ay nalungkot dahil madalas na nila noong napag-uusapan ng Kapuso actress ukol sa kanilang future plans ni Dominic.

“Isa pang balita na talagang nakalungkot, sumindak sa akin habang ako ay nasa Hong Kong ay ang balita pong naghiwalay na sina Bea Alonzo at Dominic Roque,” panimula ni Tito Boy.

BEA ALONZO NO LONGER WEARING ENGAGEMENT RING FROM DOMINIC ROQUE

True to Boy Abunda’s revelation yesterday on his “Fast Talk with Boy Abunda” show, Kapuso star Bea Alonzo was spotted not wearing her engagement ring, which, according to Boy, the actress has already returned to Dominic Roque.










At last Thursday’s launch of OPPO Reno11 5G and the OPPO Reno11 Pro 5G where Bea is the brand ambassador alongside Nadine Lustre, the “One More Chance” actress gamely answered the press' questions about the supposed wedding and plans after it.

In an interview with Nelson Canlas for “24 Oras” days after the engagement, Bea described the classic diamond engagement ring as “perfect.” She disclosed that she was expecting the wedding to take place this 2024.

PANOORIN! PAHAYAG NG MGA NETIZENS TUNGKOL SA ISSUE

Makikita rito ang ulat ng isang Tiktok star na si Johnreymar Yruma kung ano ang kanyang pananaw sa nasabing issue.


Boy and fellow talent manager Ogie Diaz, however, said that while Bea and Dominic have split and the wedding planning has been put into a halt, the couple are still trying to sort things out. The pair has also not personally addressed the issue or released a public statement as of press time.

Maybe, there could be a real-life “A Second Chance” for Bea and Dominic, after all?
